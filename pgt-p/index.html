PGT-P

OVERVIEW

PGT-P, preimplantation genetic testing for polygenic disorders, is for couples wanting more information about the chance their children will have an increased risk for specific polygenic conditions. Polygenic disorders are diseases influenced by genetic variants in more than one gene. The combination of variants in these several genes affects the likelihood of developing polygenic conditions over the course of an individual's lifetime. Genomic Prediction is proud to be the first company to offer this option to families. Couples have the ability to choose which polygenic conditions they want included in their screening from the list below.

Polygenic conditions available for screening:,

  • Type 1 and Type 2 Diabetes
  • Breast Cancer, Ovarian Cancer, Testicular Cancer, Prostate Cancer, Malignant Melanoma and Basal Cell Carcinoma
  • Coronary Artery Disease, Heart Attack Risk, Hypercholesterolemia, Hypertension, Resistive Hypertension and Atrial Fibrillation
  • Intellectual Disability
  • Idiopathic Short Stature

PROCESS

  1. Genetic Counseling:
    1. If the case is approved, a genetic counseling appointment to discuss the benefits and limitations of PGT-P will be scheduled.
    2. Our genetic counselor will facilitate collection of parental samples which are used to aid in data interpretation for improved accuracy.
    3. To learn more about our Genetic Counseling services, click here.
  2. Embryo Biopsy and Reporting:
    1. Once the parental samples are received in the Genomic Prediction Clinical Laboratory, biopsies can be sent for testing.
    2. Embryos created without intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI) are subject to DNA contamination by other sperm, and cannot be accepted for PGT-P testing.
    3. PGT-P is performed and results are available within two weeks in our provider portal.
    4. PGT-A via SNP technology is included at no additional charge.
